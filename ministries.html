Small Groups

Join a small group! Small groups meet once a week and are an opportunity to go more in-depth into the Bible with one another. If you are interested in joining a small group or learning more about small groups please email brianf@berkeley.edu

All Classes Small Group (Thurs 7-9pm) - Brian Fu, Ben Leong, Katie Chen

All Classes Small Group is for everyone! Whether you are already strong in your faith or just want to learn more about the Bible, this small group is a great place where you can do so and find fellowship. If you are interested, please reach out to us for location details or if you have any question!

Newcomer's Small Group (Mon 8-10pm) - Josh Quan, Hannah Zhuang

Newcomer's small group is specifically tailored to newcomers on campus, such as freshman or junior transfers. This is a great place to meet other people new to Cal and to bond and learn about the Bible together! For any questions or if you just want to chat about small group just email us!

SOWER

SOWER stands for Serving in Outreach, Worship, Encouragement, and Relationship-building. Each ministry seeks to meet the needs of the fellowship from a different angle. Together, they provide a strong foundation for the community here at AACF.
